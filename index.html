About Us

Think Global, Act Local.

NayePankh Foundation is a non-governmental organisation with a strong desire to help society and make it a better place for all. Service to mankind is the service to God.

Founded during the tough times of COVID-19, we later expanded our operations to provide help to a wider section of society — giving wings (naye pankh) to the underprivileged.

Our Work Get in Touch
What We Do

Uplift. Empower. Transform.

We are one of the biggest student-led NGOs in India, with operations in Kanpur, Ghaziabad, and various other cities.

🍱

Food Distribution

Free meals for the underprivileged, street children, and stray animals — because no one should sleep hungry.

📚

Education

Providing educational support and resources to underprivileged children to secure their future.

🩺

Health & Hygiene

Distributing sanitary napkins and running awareness campaigns about menstrual hygiene across communities.

👗

Clothing Drives

Collecting and distributing clothes to those in need, especially during winter months.

💼

Internship Program

Building practical skills for 30,000+ young volunteers through meaningful, purpose-driven internships.

😊

Happiness Drives

Creating moments of joy for street children through play, gifts, and community celebrations.

"If we all do something, then together there is no problem that we cannot solve!"
